Ultimate SFF — Reader Demo

Three ways to map your science-fiction & fantasy reading: rank the ultimate series, or pick the ultimate authors in Sci-Fi and Fantasy — then mark what you've read and see it on a publication timeline. Everything runs locally; your picks are saved on this machine.

Swipe or use the buttons. → Ultimate · ← Pass · ↓ Skip · ⌘Z undo. Card shows the first book's cover.

For every series you flagged ULTIMATE, click each cover you've read. A green outline means read; red means not yet — so the last green book is where you stopped.
